◆ PitchVariance

float Terraria.Audio.SoundStyle.PitchVariance
getset

The pitch offset randomness value. Cannot be negative.
With Pitch at 0.0, and PitchVariance at 1.0, used pitch will range from -0.5 to 0.5.

In XNA and FNA, Pitch ranges from -1.0f (down one octave) to 1.0f (up one octave). 0.0f is unity (normal) pitch.
